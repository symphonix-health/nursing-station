# Clinical risk management plan

The safety scope covers patient selection, observation scoring and escalation, task and care-plan ownership, medication administration, handover accountability, clinical privacy, authorization, concurrency, and audit integrity. `HAZARD_LOG.md` is the controlled hazard record and `CLINICAL_SAFETY_CASE_REPORT.md` states the Phase 1 argument and deployment boundary.

Hazards are reviewed whenever a requirement, scoring threshold, patient banner, medication rule, state transition, role rule, persistence model, or integration boundary changes. The proving focused test must be updated before or with the implementation. A failed safety guard is fixed in implementation; it is not skipped or rebaselined without an approved requirement change.

Residual-risk acceptance belongs to the appointed Clinical Safety Officer and local deploying organisation. Phase 1 proves development controls over fictional seed data only.
