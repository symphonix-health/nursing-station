# Data protection impact assessment draft

Phase 1 processes seeded patient-identifiable inpatient nursing records. It therefore exercises access control, audit, minimisation, shared-screen privacy, retention, correction, and breach-investigation controls. The seeded people are fictional and are marked as such in the database. A production deployment requires controller-specific lawful-basis, country overlay, retention schedule, processor inventory, transfer assessment, patient notice, and DPO approval.
