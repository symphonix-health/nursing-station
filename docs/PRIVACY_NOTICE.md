# Phase 2 privacy notice

This build contains fictional seeded inpatient records for development and verification. Phase 2 also retrieves governed fictional context from seeded PICIS, LIS, PACS/RIS, pharmacy-system, and blood-transfusion services through BulletTrain. It does not exchange records with live clinical systems. HMIS submissions contain approved ward counts and exclude patient identifiers and free text.

Access is authenticated and scoped by tenant, ward, role, and assignment. Shared-screen privacy mode masks direct identifiers on the ward board. Clinical mutations create tamper-evident audit events. Do not enter real patient data into this development and verification environment.

A production controller must publish its own lawful-basis notice, data-subject rights route, processor and transfer details, retention schedule, contact route, and country-specific overlay. This notice does not authorise clinical deployment.
