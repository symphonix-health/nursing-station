# Phase 1 privacy notice

This build contains fictional seeded inpatient records for development and verification. It processes names, identifiers, demographics, clinical observations, medicines, assessments, care plans, task ownership, handovers, and user activity. The purpose is to prove the standalone nursing workflow and its controls. It does not exchange records with live clinical systems.

Access is authenticated and scoped by tenant, ward, role, and assignment. Shared-screen privacy mode masks direct identifiers on the ward board. Clinical mutations create tamper-evident audit events. Do not enter real patient data into the Phase 1 environment.

A production controller must publish its own lawful-basis notice, data-subject rights route, processor and transfer details, retention schedule, contact route, and country-specific overlay. The Phase 1 notice does not authorise clinical deployment.
